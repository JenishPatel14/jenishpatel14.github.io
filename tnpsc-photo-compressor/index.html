Why Is Photo Compression Important for TNPSC Applications?

TNPSC has strict guidelines for application photos, including:

  • File Size: The photo must be between 10 KB and 20 KB.
  • Dimensions: The photo should be 3.5 cm x 4.5 cm.
  • Format: The image must be in JPG or PNG format.

Using our TNPSC Photo Compressor, you can ensure your photo meets all these requirements, avoiding rejection due to incorrect photo specifications.
